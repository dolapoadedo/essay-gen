Additional Essay Questions


What’s one thing you’ve done that made you really happy?
(Keeps it positive and easy to start with.)

Who’s someone you look up to, and why?
(Simple way to talk about influence without overthinking.)

What’s a time you messed up but learned something?
(Takes the pressure off perfection, keeps it relatable.)

What do you like to do when you’re not at school?
(Opens the door to hobbies or passions, no stress.)

What’s something you wish people knew about you?
(Lets them share anything without need)
